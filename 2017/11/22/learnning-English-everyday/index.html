看不懂单词集锦

11.22

    adj可预见的,可预测的.
    eg:a predictable result
  2. predicate
    vt使基于
    eg:Financial success is usually predicated on having money or being able to obtain it.
    想要有钱或者能弄到钱才能赚钱.
    n谓语,述语
